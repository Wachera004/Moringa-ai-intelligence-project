This is a simple expense tracker designed to help users track their spending and gauge which categories they spend the most money on.

Instructions for Use:

Scroll to the Salary section, input an amount, select a date and click the Add Salary button. The inputted amount will automatically update your Balance and Income sections at the top.

Scroll to the Add New Transaction section, input the amount and date and select a category from the dropdown menu. The amount will appear in the Expense section and your Balance will decrease accordingly.

The History section helps users gauge which categories account for their highest expenses, allowing them to identify areas where they can save money.
